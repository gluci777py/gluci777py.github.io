Community Guidelines

Community Rules

Effective from the date of your first access

The GavricFX community is built for serious traders who want real results. These rules exist to protect the quality of the environment and ensure everyone gets maximum value from the mentorship. By joining, you agree to follow these guidelines.

01
Respect Every Member
Treat all members, including Gavric and staff, with respect at all times. No insults, personal attacks, or condescending behaviour. We are all here to learn and grow.
02
No Sharing of Content
Strategies, videos, alerts, trade setups, and all materials are strictly private. Do not share, screenshot, or distribute any content outside of this community. This is a zero-tolerance rule.
03
No Solicitation or Promotion
Do not promote other trading courses, signal services, or any external products within this community. Do not recruit members for competing services or your own ventures.
04
Take Responsibility for Your Trades
All trade alerts and analysis are educational only. You are solely responsible for every trade you place. Do not blame the mentorship or Gavric for losses — trading carries risk and results are never guaranteed.
05
Stay On Topic
Keep discussions relevant to trading, markets, and the mentorship. Off-topic spam, irrelevant links, or excessive self-promotion will result in a warning or removal.
06
Ask Questions Freely
There are no stupid questions. The community is a safe space to ask anything — whether you're a beginner or experienced. Gavric and senior members are here to help.
07
Be Patient with Results
Trading is a skill that takes time. Do not expect overnight results. Trust the process, follow the strategy consistently, and results will follow. Impatience leads to poor decisions.
08
Keep Account Details Private
Never share your trading account credentials, personal financial details, or passwords with anyone — including Gavric or staff. We will never ask for them.
Zero Tolerance — Instant Removal
Sharing or leaking any mentorship content externally
Harassment, threats, or abusive behaviour toward any member
Attempting to scam or deceive members in any way
Recruiting members for other services or communities
Creating fake accounts or sharing your access credentials
The GavricFX Mindset

This community is for people who are serious about trading as a skill and a discipline. We don't chase quick wins — we build lasting, consistent edge in the markets.

Show up consistently. Apply the strategy. Ask questions when you're stuck. Help others when you can. That's how you get the most out of this mentorship.

Gavric is personally invested in your success. But success requires effort from your side too.
